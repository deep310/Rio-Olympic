Swimming

A breath-taking sport, swimming has been in the Olympic Games since Athens 1896.

Men and women participate in 16 events, including relays and individual competitions in four strokes.

freestyle, breaststroke, butterfly and backstroke.

Aim of the Game

Swimmers must complete a set distance inside the pool.

In some cases using a specific swimming style, in the shortest possible time.

Why should you watch this?

Chad le Clos, Missy Franklin, Michael Phelps...

The Rio 2016 Olympic swimming pool will be overflowing with world-class stars, which is a recipe for record-breaking thrills.
