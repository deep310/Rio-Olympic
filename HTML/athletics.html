Athletics

Running, jumping and throwing. One of the most traditional Olympic sports.

Athletics has been part of the Games since Athens 1896, the first of the modern era.

Today, no other sport offers more medals: 141, 47 of which are gold.

Aim of the Game

Across the different events the aim is always the same:

To outperform your opponents, whether in running, jumping or throwing.

Why should you watch this?

Some of the greatest athletes of all time, such as Usain Bolt,

will strive to achieve unprecedented feats amid a carnival of sporting challenges that encompass the Olympic motto:

Faster, Higher, Stronger.
